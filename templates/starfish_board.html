Starfish Retrospective

This retrospective board is structured into five sections, each symbolizing a distinct strategy for team reflection:

  • Start: Pinpoint new practices or approaches the team should adopt.
  • Stop: Identify behaviors or processes that are hindering progress and should be ceased.
  • Keep Doing: Acknowledge the current actions that are yielding positive results and should be carried on.
  • Do More: Determine what practices are working and should be intensified.
  • Do Less: Recognize what is being overdone and should be reduced.

Engage with each section by adding a sticky note to share your thoughts. Move and place the sticky notes freely to emphasize your points.

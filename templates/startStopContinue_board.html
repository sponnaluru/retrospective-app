Start Stop Continue Retrospective

This retrospective board enables a straightforward review of team practices with three clear prompts:

  • Start: Enumerate the activities or strategies the team should begin employing.
  • Stop: Call out any counterproductive or unnecessary practices that need to be halted.
  • Continue: Reaffirm the valuable actions that contribute positively to the team's work and should persist.

Interact with each section using sticky notes to voice your suggestions, arranging them to signal their importance or sequence.

Sailboat Retrospective

The Sailboat Retrospective board is visually themed to resemble a journey at sea, segmented into different concepts:

  • Wind: Consider what is propelling the team forward.
  • Anchors: Reflect on what is holding the team back.
  • Rocks: Anticipate potential risks on the horizon.
  • Island: Define the ultimate goals and objectives towards which the team is sailing.

Add your observations as sticky notes within each category. Position them to illustrate their impact on the team's voyage.

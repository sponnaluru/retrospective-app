Plus Delta Retrospective

The Plus Delta Retrospective board is focused on continuous improvement, broken down into two main categories:

  • Plus (What worked well): Discuss the successful strategies and why they should be maintained.
  • Delta (Opportunities for improvement): Identify what could be changed or improved for better outcomes in future sprints.

Use sticky notes to contribute your insights and rearrange them as needed to prioritize the team's reflections.
